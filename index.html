All About Hair

Hair Is The Second Fastest Growing Tissue In The Body.

Yes If You Didn't Know That Your Fastest Growing Tissue In Your Body Is Your Hair. Only To The Bone Marrow. The Average Scalp Has 100,000 Hairs . Red Headers Has About 80,000. Brown & Black Has About 100,000. And Blondes Has About 120,000 Hairs .

At Different Times , 90% Of Your Hair Is Growing , While The Other 10% Is Resting.

During The Day Your Hair Is Pushing Hairs That Will Continue To Grow Until They're Cut Or Until They Reach The End Of Their Lifespan And Fall Out . And The Other 10-15% Are Not Active Or Growing

A Single Strand Of Hair Has A Lifespan Of About Three To Five Years

Growing Phase A Single Hair Has A Normal Life Between 2-7 Years. Hair Then Falls Out And Is Replaced With A New Hair. It Is The Longest Phase, Though For Some People A Single Hair Could Continue Growing For 7 Or More Years.

Hair Grows Faster In Warm Weather , Things To Help Hair Grow In The Summer

Human Hair Indeed Grows Faster During The Summer By About 10% Compared To Hair Growth During The Colder Weather And Slower In The Winter.

When Do Your Hair Stop Growing?

Many People Hair Stops To Grow At The Age Of 30 . Many Men Lose All Their Hair Around 60 Years Old And Women Lose Their Hair Around 40-50 Years Old. Your hair still can grow but the growth will Be slower and get thinner also lighter.
